Here we considered the approach of insertion sort, which is an efficient algorithm for sorting a small number of elements. Insertion sort works the way many people sort a hand of playing cards. We start with an empty right box (red) and the cards face down on the left box (yellow). We then add one card at a time from the left box and insert it into the correct position in the right box. To find the correct position for a card, we compare it with each of the cards already in the right box, from left to right, as illustrated in the given problem, all times, the cards held in the right box are sorted.
